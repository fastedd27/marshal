# contradiction-checks (template)

> Internal inconsistencies to catch, not just blanks. Rule of thumb from the field: only check
> contradictions the record actually captures the data for, and that hold regardless of vendor,
> tool, or brand. Skip anything that assumes a value the record does not hold.

## The checks

- `[item A claims a relationship with item B, but item B is not documented]`
- `[a sub-record references something the parent record never lists]`
- `[two fields that should agree but do not]`
- `[PLACEHOLDER]`

Each contradiction the author can fix returns to the author with the specific conflict named.

## Not checks (deliberately excluded)

- `[anything that needs a capacity, spec, or value the record does not capture]`
- `[anything that varies by vendor or tool and so cannot be judged reliably]`
